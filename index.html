1. Who We Are & Scope

HoppiBee is a multilingual language learning platform designed for users of all ages — from toddlers to adults. Our mission is to make language learning engaging, effective, and accessible to everyone.

This Privacy Policy applies to the HoppiBee mobile application available on iOS and Android, and to any companion web presence operated by HoppiBee. It describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with your use of our services.

By using HoppiBee, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide, improve, and personalise the HoppiBee learning experience. The categories below describe what we collect and why.

(a) Account Data — Registered Users
When you create a HoppiBee account, we collect your email address and an authentication identifier via Firebase Authentication. This is used solely to identify your account and secure access to your saved progress.
(b) Anonymous Session Identifier — Guest Users
If you choose to use HoppiBee as a guest (without registering), we assign an anonymous session identifier via Firebase Authentication's anonymous sign-in feature. No name, email address, age, or any personal detail is required or collected. Guest usage requires zero personal information.
(c) Learning Progress Data
We store your selected learning language, completed lessons, quiz scores, XP points, streak count, earned badges, and identified strengths and weaknesses. This data is always saved on your device. For registered users it is additionally synced to Firebase Cloud Firestore so your progress is available across devices.
(d) Crash & Performance Diagnostics
We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash reports and performance data when the app encounters an error. This data helps us identify and fix bugs. It does not contain personally identifiable information.
(e) Usage Analytics
We use Firebase Analytics to collect aggregate, anonymised data about how users interact with the app — such as which lessons are started or completed, quiz performance trends, and language selection patterns. We do not collect verbatim event logs tied to individual identities.
(f) Advertising Data
HoppiBee displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob is configured with child-directed treatment for all users — meaning only contextual, non-personalised ads are served. No interest-based or behavioural advertising identifiers are used. This applies to both guest and registered users.
(g) Push Notification Tokens
If you enable push notifications, we collect a device notification token via Firebase Cloud Messaging. This token is used only to send you learning reminders and app updates. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

3. Children's Privacy

HoppiBee is designed for users of all ages, including young children, and contains no violent, harmful, or age-inappropriate content. We take children's privacy seriously and have built the app with data minimisation as a core principle.

Guest Mode — Zero Personal Data: Any user — regardless of age — can use HoppiBee in guest mode without providing any personal information whatsoever. Guest mode collects only an anonymous session identifier. No name, email, age, or any other personal detail is required or stored.

Children Under 13 (COPPA): The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) requires verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information from children under 13 in the United States. A formal COPPA-compliant verifiable parental consent flow is not yet present in the current version of HoppiBee and is planned for a future release.

In the interim, we recommend the following:

  • Children under 13 should use HoppiBee in guest mode, which requires no personal information and keeps all data on the device.
  • If a parent or guardian wishes to create a registered account that syncs progress to the cloud, they should create the account themselves using their own email address and supervise the child's use of the app.
  • Parental supervision is recommended for all children using any digital application, including HoppiBee.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13 without appropriate consent, please contact us immediately at hoppiebeeadmin@gmail.com and we will promptly delete the information.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Deliver and personalise learning content — to present lessons, quizzes, and exercises appropriate to your selected language, progress level, and identified strengths and weaknesses.
  • Save and sync your progress — to store completed lessons, quiz scores, XP, streaks, and badges on your device, and (for registered users) to sync this progress to the cloud so it is available across multiple devices.
  • Improve the app — to identify and fix crashes via diagnostic data, and to understand aggregate usage patterns via anonymised analytics so we can make HoppiBee better for all users.
  • Serve contextual ads — to display relevant, non-personalised advertisements via Google AdMob. All ads use child-directed treatment; no interest-based targeting is applied to any user.
  • Send push notifications — to send learning reminders, streak alerts, and app update notices, but only when you have enabled notifications on your device. You can opt out at any time via your device settings.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for purposes beyond those listed above.

6. Data Storage & Security

On-device storage: All users — including guests — have their learning progress saved locally on their device using the device's secure local storage capabilities. This data is available immediately without an internet connection.

Cloud storage (registered users only): When you create a registered account, your progress is additionally stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore and your profile assets may be stored in Firebase Storage. This enables progress to be synced across multiple devices.

Security practices: We apply industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit — all communication between the app and Firebase services uses TLS/HTTPS.
  • Encryption at rest — Firebase encrypts data stored on its servers.
  • Firestore security rules — each registered user's progress data in Firestore is protected by security rules that permit access only by the authenticated user who owns that data. No other user, and no unauthenticated request, can read or write your progress documents.
  • Authentication — all registered-user data operations require a valid Firebase Authentication session tied to your account.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

7. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information associated with your use of HoppiBee.

Guest Users

All data for guest sessions exists only on your device. To permanently remove it, uninstall the HoppiBee app or clear the app's data through your device's Settings. No action is required from us — there is no cloud-stored data to delete for guest accounts.

Registered Users

To delete your registered account and all associated data, use the in-app deletion flow:

  1. Open HoppiBee and navigate to your Profile.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your identity by re-authenticating with your password or Google account.
  4. Confirm the deletion. Your Firebase Authentication account and all associated Firestore data (profile, progress, quiz scores, badges) will be permanently deleted.

You may also submit a data access or deletion request by contacting us at hoppiebeeadmin@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

Active accounts: We retain your account and learning progress data for as long as your account remains active. You can review and update your information at any time through the app's Profile section.

After account deletion: When you delete your account (via the in-app flow described in Section-7), all personal data stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore — including your profile, learning progress, quiz scores, streaks, and badges — is permanently purged within 30 days of the deletion request.

Analytics data: Anonymised or aggregated analytics data collected via Firebase Analytics — which cannot be linked back to any individual user — may be retained beyond the 30-day window for product improvement purposes.

Guest data: Data stored on-device for guest sessions is removed immediately when the app is uninstalled or when the app's data is cleared through device settings. No cloud deletion is necessary.
